Amm Sawaalo ke Jawaab

1.Online booking safe hai?

Haan, online booking safe hai. Hum secure data protection ke liye latest technology ka istemaal karte hain.

2.Kaise booking cancel kar sakte hain?

Aap 'Your Booked Salon' button mai click kare phir uske under booking cancel kar sakte hain. ab kabhi bhi cancel kar sakte hai.

3.Kya payment online hi karna zaroori hai?

Nahi, aap salon par cash se payment kar sakte hain.

4.Kitna time pehle booking karni chahiye?

10 minute pehle booking karna behtar hai, lekin last minute slots mai bhi booking kar sakte hain.

5.Agar salon late ho jaye to kya hoga?

Agar aap 5 minute se zyada late hote hain, toh appointment cancel ho sakti hai. Salon se confirm karen.

6.Naye salon kaise register kar sakte hain?

Ab Shayan Shahid se Baat Kare.

10.Agar koi problem ho to support kaise mil sakta hai?

i.Ab salon owner ke number me call kar sakte hai

ii.Hamare website ke 'Contact Us' section se humse contact kar sakte hain ya email pe support team se baat kar sakte hain.
